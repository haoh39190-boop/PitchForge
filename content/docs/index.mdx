## What is PitchForge?

PitchForge is an AI automotive sales script and customer follow-up tool. It helps you organize customer profiles, generate follow-up language, retain communication records, and build a personal library of saved scripts.

## Get started

1. Open **Customers** and create a customer.
2. Add the customer name, vehicle of interest, and budget.
3. Complete priorities, main concerns, and notes from the customer overview.
4. Open **Generate follow-up** and choose the stage, channel, and tone.
5. Edit the result, rewrite it, or apply additional guidance.
6. Copy the language when you need to use it. Select **Save record** when the final version should be added to the customer’s communication history.

## Saved scripts

Strong versions can be saved for later. Saved scripts are an independent personal library and do not automatically affect new generation. They are only applied when you choose one for a customer.

## Language and theme

Use the controls in the upper-right corner to switch between Chinese and English, and between light, dark, or system themes. Your preference is retained in the current browser.

## Data storage

Customer profiles, communication records, and saved scripts remain in the current browser. Clearing site data or moving to another browser will not transfer them automatically.
