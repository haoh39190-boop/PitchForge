## How data is stored

PitchForge keeps customer profiles, communication records, and saved scripts in the browser you are currently using. The product does not require an account and does not automatically sync this business content to a remote account.

## Browser permissions

When you use a copy action, the browser may request clipboard permission. This permission is used only to copy the selected script.

## Your choices

You can delete customers, communication records, and saved scripts from the product at any time. You can also use your browser’s site data controls to clear all PitchForge content stored locally.

## Important considerations

- Clearing browser site data may permanently remove saved content.
- Data does not automatically transfer to another browser or device.
- Avoid placing unrelated sensitive personal information in customer notes.

## Policy updates

This page will be updated if the product’s data handling changes.
